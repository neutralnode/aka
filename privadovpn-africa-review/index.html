PrivadoVPN for Africa — detailed review

The price advantage: At $1.11/month, PrivadoVPN is genuinely the cheapest paid VPN available globally. For African users where every dollar matters, this is a real advantage. The free plan at 10GB/month on unlimited devices is also better than most free options — though ProtonVPN free with unlimited data is still our recommendation for free users.

The Africa limitation: PrivadoVPN has only one server location in Africa — compared to Surfshark's three (Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa). For streaming African content or reducing latency, this is a significant disadvantage. PrivadoVPN works better as a privacy and security tool than as a streaming or geo-unblocking tool for African users.

Obfuscation: PrivadoVPN has basic obfuscation but it is less robust than Surfshark NoBorders or ProtonVPN Stealth. For high-censorship environments (Egypt, Ethiopia, Uganda), we recommend Surfshark or ProtonVPN instead.
