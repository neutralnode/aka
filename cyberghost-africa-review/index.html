CyberGhost for Africa — detailed review

45-day guarantee: This is CyberGhost's strongest card for African users. You have 45 days to test it and get a full refund if it does not meet your needs. For someone trying a paid VPN for the first time, this removes all financial risk.

African servers: CyberGhost has servers in Nigeria, South Africa and Kenya — a solid African coverage. The servers are optimised for streaming, which makes it a good choice for DStv, Africa Magic and BBNaija.

Limitations: CyberGhost limits connections to 7 devices simultaneously — less generous than Surfshark's unlimited policy. Its obfuscation (NoSpy servers) is less robust than Surfshark NoBorders or ProtonVPN Stealth — not recommended for Egypt or high-DPI environments. No free plan.
