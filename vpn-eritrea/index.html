Why do you need a VPN in Eritrea?

Eritrea has the most isolated internet in Africa — and arguably the world outside North Korea. Mobile internet access is blocked for ordinary citizens. The country has no submarine fiber-optic cable landings, making it the only coastal African nation completely cut off from undersea infrastructure. As of 2025, roughly 80% of Eritreans have never been online. Those who do access the internet must use slow connections at a handful of internet cafés in Asmara.

Eritrea continuously blocks all social media — the only African country with a permanent blanket social media ban. A VPN with advanced obfuscation like ProtonVPN Stealth or Surfshark NoBorders is the only realistic way to access blocked content. The limited connectivity means VPN performance will be slow regardless — but a VPN is essential for privacy and access to any restricted content.
